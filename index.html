Brand-Strategy:

We don't just give you an internet presence, we also take your company from a business to a fully fledged brand by creating a brand strategy.
A brand-strategy is a term used for a set of tools that group together two fundamental properties of a business; a brand-identity and a company policy.

The first component is the brand-identity or brand-image.A brand identity is a consumers perception of a brand derived from visual and aural elements of the company. These elements are used in conjunction, to form an identity or character that a potential client can instantly relate to.
