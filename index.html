Rock School:

Designed around the Trinity College of London's Rock and Pop syllabus (2018-2024).



The Rock School syllabus focuses on merging the techniques required for both acoustic and electric guitar. The aim of the syllabus is to build a skill set that will be useful in building a repertoire that suits both instruments,

with an emphasis on a variety of finger and plectrum techniques. Students will be encouraged to explore a wide range of styles including, rock-and-pop, jazz, blues, and country. The syllabus also covers chord progressions and patterns, along with standard and none-standard, scales and arpeggios, incorporating the use of both plectrum and fingers.
